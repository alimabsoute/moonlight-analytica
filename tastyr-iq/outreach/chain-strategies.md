# Tastyr IQ — Social Chain Interaction Maps

## Philosophy

Cold outreach converts at ~2%. Warm intros convert at ~40%. The difference is the chain. Every investor meeting should feel like a natural conversation, not a pitch — because by the time Ali sits down, the investor has already heard about Tastyr IQ from 2-3 trusted sources.

---

## Chain A: "The Restaurateur Gateway"

### Goal
Use Philadelphia's food scene to create organic investor interest through restaurateur champions.

### Chain Links
```
Step 1: Food Blogger → Posts dish score content
        ↓
Step 2: Restaurateur → Sees coverage, gets curious
        ↓
Step 3: Restaurateur → Becomes champion/early adopter
        ↓
Step 4: Restaurateur → Tells investor friends at industry dinner
        ↓
Step 5: Investor → Hears from TRUSTED source, takes Ali's call
```

### Detailed Execution

**Step 1: Activate Food Blogger**
- Target: 2-3 Philly food Instagram accounts (10K+ followers)
- Action: Give exclusive beta access, suggest they rate dishes at 2-3 popular restaurants
- Timeline: Week 1
- Success metric: At least 1 post with 500+ engagement

**Step 2: Restaurateur Sees Buzz**
- Wait for organic curiosity OR proactively reach out
- Message: "Your [specific dish] scored 94/100 on Tastyr IQ. Thought you'd want to know."
- Include screenshot of the score
- Timeline: Week 1-2

**Step 3: Onboard Restaurateur**
- Offer "Founding Cohort" status
- Give dashboard access to see all dish scores
- Ask permission to use their name/restaurant in materials
- Timeline: Week 2-3

**Step 4: Leverage Restaurateur Network**
- Ask: "Do you know any investors who care about the food industry?"
- Restaurateurs naturally talk to their investors, bankers, advisors
- Industry events (James Beard dinners, etc.) are where this happens
- Timeline: Week 3-4

**Step 5: Warm Investor Intro**
- Investor already knows about Tastyr from trusted restaurateur
- Ali's pitch starts with: "[Restaurateur Name] suggested we connect"
- Credibility is pre-built
- Timeline: Week 4-6

## Chain C: "The FOMO Cascade"

### Goal
Create perception of unstoppable momentum through coordinated buzz across multiple channels simultaneously.

### Chain Links
```
Step 1: Seed 5-10 influencers with exclusive beta access simultaneously
        ↓
Step 2: Coordinated posts → "Everyone's talking about this" perception
        ↓
Step 3: Media picks up the trend → Headlines create legitimacy
        ↓
Step 4: Restaurateurs scramble → "How is my restaurant not on here?"
        ↓
Step 5: Investors see market pull → Ali pitches from position of strength
```

### Detailed Execution

**Step 1: Seed Influencers (Day -7 to Day 0)**
- Select 5-10 influencers across platforms: Instagram (3-4), TikTok (2-3), YouTube (1-2)
- Each gets personalized onboarding with "exclusive" framing
- Brief them on what makes Tastyr unique (dish scores, not restaurant reviews)
- DON'T tell them others are posting at the same time — let it feel organic
- Provide a "posting guide" (not a script — authenticity matters)

**Step 2: Coordinated Drop (Day 0-2)**
- All influencers post within 48-hour window
- Ali reposts/engages with every piece of content
- Tastyr IQ's own social channels amplify each post
- Result: Anyone in the Philly food scene sees Tastyr IQ 3-5 times in 2 days

**Step 3: Media Pitch (Day 2-4)**
- Pitch angle: "New AI app has Philly food influencers buzzing"
- Provide 5+ influencer posts as evidence of "trend"
- Offer exclusive deeper story to one outlet (Eater Philly ideal)
- Backup: pitch Technical.ly for tech angle

**Step 4: Restaurateur FOMO (Day 4-10)**
- Restaurateurs see their dishes being rated publicly
- Proactively reach out: "Your competitors' dishes are being scored — want to see yours?"
- Create urgency: "Our founding cohort is filling up"

**Step 5: Investor Position of Strength (Day 10-20)**
- By now, investor has seen: influencer posts, media coverage, restaurateur interest
- Ali's pitch: "We're experiencing organic market pull — here's the data"
- Investor FOMO: "If I don't invest, the next investor will"

- **Media**: Eater Philly editor, Technical.ly reporter, PhillyMag food editor
- **Restaurateurs**: 5-10 from founding cohort
- **Investors**: All Tier 1 contacts (rank 90-100)

### Contingency
If influencer adoption is slow:
- Offer paid partnerships (small amounts, $200-500 per post)
- Create "Tastyr IQ challenge" with prize (best dish discovery)
- Reach out to more influencers (cast wider net)

```
Week 3-4:  Chain A Restaurateur Gateway produces first champions

Week 4-6:  All chains producing warm intros
           Tier 1 investor meetings happening
           Media coverage stacking

Week 6-8:  Chain H Connector Express amplifies all other chains
           Multiple investor conversations in parallel

Week 8-12: Term sheet conversations
           Chains mature into ongoing relationships
           Close round
```

## Chain Health Monitoring

| Chain | Green Signal | Red Signal |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| A: Restaurateur | 3+ restaurants engaged | 0 restaurants after 3 weeks |
| B: University | Faculty partnership formed | No faculty response |
| C: FOMO Cascade | 5+ influencer posts | < 2 posts after 2 weeks |
| D: NJ-NYC Bridge | NJ angel meeting scheduled | No NJ traction after 4 weeks |
| E: Accelerator | Accepted to 1+ program | Rejected from all |
| F: Food Industry | POS/distributor interested | No industry interest |
| G: Media | 1+ article published | No media response |
| H: Connector | 5+ warm intros received | < 2 intros after 3 weeks |

**Rule**: If a chain shows Red, don't abandon it — pivot the approach. If 3+ chains show Red simultaneously, reassess the overall strategy.
