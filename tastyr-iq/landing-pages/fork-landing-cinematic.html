AI Scoring to Give You a Definitive Win

Every dish gets a score. Not the restaurant.

A 4.5-star restaurant can serve a 6/10 biryani. A no-name spot can serve a 94. Our patent-pending algorithm extracts dish-specific attributes and scores each plate 0–100. No star ratings. No guesswork.

  • Ramen scored on broth richness, noodle firmness, chashu, egg quality
  • Pizza scored on leopard spotting, cornicione puff, dough fermentation
  • Sushi scored on fish freshness, rice quality, itamae knife technique
  • Ethiopian scored on berbere depth, injera sourness, sauce richness
  • Spider charts show exactly where each dish wins and loses
