The Algorithm

The math behind every score.

Our AI analyzes thousands of reviews across every restaurant in your city and scores each dish individually. Every plate gets its own score — unique to that dish, not the restaurant.

Weights tuned per dish.

Not every attribute matters equally. For ramen, broth depth is everything. For carbonara, the no-cream test trumps presentation. We tune the weights per dish so the score reflects what actually makes that plate great.

The result is a single number you can trust — and a spider chart that shows you exactly why.
