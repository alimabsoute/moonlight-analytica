Problem Section — Enhanced Pain Points

Hover lift with animated left border. Numbered badges. Card background on hover. Staggered entrance animations.

Restaurant ratings are broken.

1

A 4.5-star restaurant can serve a 2-star dish.

Restaurant ratings are averages of averages. The sushi might be incredible but the pasta is terrible. A single star rating can't tell you what to actually order.

2

You've ordered the wrong thing. Everyone has.

You pick a great restaurant, sit down, and choose the wrong dish off a 40-item menu. You leave disappointed — not because the restaurant was bad, but because nobody told you what was actually good there.

3

Yelp reviews are everyone else's opinion.

You like extra spice. You hate cilantro. You care about portion size. But aggregate ratings don't know that. Every review you read is someone else's taste — not yours.
