Mission District Still Leads

Despite predictions from food media that the Mission's taqueria scene would be displaced by rising costs and neighborhood change, the data doesn't support this narrative. The Mission District still contains the highest concentration of high-scoring taquerias in the city, and the gap between the Mission average (74.2) and the citywide average (65.8) has remained stable across our 14-month measurement window. The critical variable is the community — a neighborhood with a high concentration of Mexican families who eat tacos regularly creates quality pressure that survives economic disruption better than other quality signals.
