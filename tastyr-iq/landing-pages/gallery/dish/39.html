The Injera at 92

The injera here is 72-hour fermented teff, prepared in-house, with a sourness level that our model scores 92 on the acidity calibration dimension — meaning it achieves the clean, lactic sour profile of properly fermented teff without the sharp or vinegary off-notes that rushed fermentation produces.
