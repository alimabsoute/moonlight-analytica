What the Comparison Shows

The data does not support the assumption that geographic prestige correlates with technical quality in omakase sushi. The highest composite score in the three-city comparison belongs to the Flushing Queens counter at 96, not to any Manhattan destination operation. Chicago's top-ranked counter at 95 outscores every Manhattan counter in our dataset. The value index gap — 99 in Chicago versus 74 for comparable-quality Manhattan operations — is the largest dimension gap we've observed across any cuisine comparison in our dataset.
