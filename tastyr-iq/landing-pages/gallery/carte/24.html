Best Pizza in Philadelphia

Philly's pizza scene is underrated nationally but deeply serious locally. South Philly red-sauce traditions, a growing Neapolitan movement, and several standout Roman-al-taglio spots create a surprisingly deep field.

Key Findings
  • Beddia's Margherita is the single most acclaimed pizza in Philly — cult following since 2016, still the gold standard
  • South Philly's tomato pie tradition (no mozzarella, sauce on top) is an authentic local style worth experiencing
  • Roman al taglio is growing fastest — 4 new spots opened in 2025
  • Value scores are notably high — Philly pizza remains 20-30% cheaper than equivalent NYC products
