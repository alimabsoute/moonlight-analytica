Chicago's Thai food scene clusters in Andersonville and Lakeview, where a generation of Thai-American chefs are reframing the dish — moving past tourist-friendly sweetness toward proper tamarind acidity and wok hai smoke.

Key Findings
  • Wok hai (breath of the wok) is Chicago's differentiator — restaurants with BTU-rated commercial woks score 14pts higher
  • Sen lek (thin rice noodles) outperform flat rice noodles in texture scores across the board
  • Tamarind-to-fish-sauce balance is the #1 quality marker — sweetness should not dominate
  • Andersonville Thai restaurants now outrank Chinatown Thai by avg 9 points
