Best Sushi in Chicago

Updated April 2026 · 10 dishes ranked · ForkFox Carte Guide

Chicago's sushi scene benefits from deep Midwest umami traditions — restaurant groups here take fermentation and curing seriously, producing omakase experiences that rival coasts at notably lower price points.

Key Findings
  • Wicker Park has quietly become the sushi neighborhood — 4 of the top 10 are within six blocks
  • Wagyu nigiri overtook fatty tuna as the prestige order in 2025
  • Chicago's cold-water fish sourcing from Great Lakes suppliers adds distinctive terroir
  • Omakase value gap vs. NYC is widening — comparable meals run $40 less on average
