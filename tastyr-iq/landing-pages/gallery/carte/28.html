Updated April 2026 · 9 dishes ranked · ForkFox Carte Guide

Key Findings
  • Japantown is the center of gravity — Hinodeya and Marufuku both have dedicated cult followings
  • Mensho Tokyo's Tenderloin location is SF's most technically ambitious bowl — house milling flour for noodles
  • Vegan ramen is better than average here — Shizen has converted meat-eaters with their mushroom version
  • Wait times are part of the SF ramen culture — top spots average 35min and it doesn't hurt their scores
