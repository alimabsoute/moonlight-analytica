Best Pho in New York

Updated April 2026 · 10 dishes ranked · ForkFox Carte Guide

New York's pho landscape is anchored in Flushing and the East Village, where Vietnamese communities have maintained decades-long broth-craft traditions. The best bowls here are architectural — layers of star anise, charred ginger, and twelve-hour bones.

Key Findings
  • Flushing bowls score 8% higher on authenticity than Manhattan counterparts
  • Broth clarity is the #1 quality signal — cloudy broth correlates with lower flavor scores
  • Pho ga (chicken) is rapidly gaining prestige — 3 entries in the top 10 this cycle
  • Average portion sizes in NYC are 15% larger than LA equivalent restaurants
