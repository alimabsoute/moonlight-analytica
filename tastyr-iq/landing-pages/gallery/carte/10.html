Birria has had its cultural moment and SF was ready for it — the Mission District absorbed the trend and elevated it. The best spots here are obsessive about adobo depth, chili layering, and consomé concentration.

Key Findings
  • Consomé quality is the most differentiating factor — depth should come from at minimum 3 chili varieties
  • Quesatacos have taken the crown over standalone tacos — the cheese layer adds textural contrast
  • SF's birria spots outperform LA equivalents on price — avg $4.50 vs $5.25 per taco
  • Goat birria is making a comeback — 2 new entries vs. none last quarter
