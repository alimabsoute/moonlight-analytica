Best Tacos in San Francisco

Updated April 2026 · 10 dishes ranked · ForkFox Carte Guide

San Francisco's taco landscape is defined by its Mission District legacy — a dense corridor of taquerias producing some of the most technically precise street tacos in the US. The competition here is fierce and the ceiling is high.

Key Findings
  • Mission District taquerias dominate the top 6 spots with consistent al pastor excellence
  • Tortilla quality separates top-tier from mid-tier — hand-pressed corn pulls ahead every time
  • Average wait times at top spots: 18 minutes — factored into value scores
  • Birria tacos rising fast — 3 new entries since last quarter's rankings
