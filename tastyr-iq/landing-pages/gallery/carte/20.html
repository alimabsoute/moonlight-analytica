Key Findings
  • Locals eat tavern-style (square-cut thin) 3:1 over deep dish — tourist perception inverts the actual market
  • Lou Malnati's butter crust remains the technical gold standard — the sausage layer is a full patty, not crumbles
  • Caramelized crust edge (Pequod's signature) scores highest on texture across all deep dish styles
  • Coal-fired Neapolitan-style pizza is growing fast — Coalfire has broken into the legacy rankings
