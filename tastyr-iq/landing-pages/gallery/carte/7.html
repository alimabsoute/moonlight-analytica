New York's pizza culture is so entrenched that it operates as its own religion. Fold technique, coal vs. gas oven, water sourcing — locals debate these with alarming seriousness. The rankings reflect what actually scores, not what gets the most tourist traffic.

Key Findings
  • Char level on the cornicione is the single best predictor of overall score — "leopard spotting" required
  • Mozzarella sourcing reveals itself immediately — low-moisture domestic scores 12 pts below hand-pulled fresh
  • The Sicilian/grandma resurgence is real — 3 square-style entries in the top 10 this cycle
  • Per-slice pricing is diverging: tourist traps average $6.50, neighborhood spots hold at $3.75
