Key Findings
  • Phoenix Restaurant remains Chicago's undisputed dim sum #1 — weekend cart service is a pilgrimage
  • Chinatown Square's newer spots are raising the bar — Ming Hin and Dim Sum King compete closely
  • Argyle Street's fusion dim sum is underrated — Vietnamese influence creates interesting flavor combinations
  • Chicago dim sum prices are 25-35% cheaper than SF or NYC — exceptional value across the board
