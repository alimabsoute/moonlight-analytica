## Ranking System (1-100)

```
RANK = (Investor Potential × 0.30) + (Network Reach × 0.25) +
       (FOMO Multiplier × 0.20) + (Accessibility × 0.15) + (Timing × 0.10)
```

- **90-100**: Contact THIS WEEK
- **70-89**: Contact within 2 weeks
- **50-69**: Contact within month 1
- **30-49**: Month 2-3
- **1-29**: Long game

## Social Chain Strategy

This campaign uses **people-link approach maps** — chains of influence rather than cold outreach:

1. **Restaurateur Gateway**: Blogger → Restaurant → Investor
2. **University Pipeline**: Faculty → Students → Alumni Angels
3. **FOMO Cascade**: Coordinated influencer posts → Media → Market pull
4. **NJ-to-NYC Bridge**: Princeton → NJ Angels → NYC VCs
