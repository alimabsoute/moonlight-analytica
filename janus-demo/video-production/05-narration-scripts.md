# Narration Scripts — All 5 Videos

## Voice Direction (All Videos)
- **Tone**: Professional, confident, approachable — not robotic or salesy
- **Pace**: 150-160 words per minute (natural conversational speed)
- **Style**: Think "Stripe product video" or "Linear engineering blog narrator"
- **Pauses**: Marked with `[pause]` — hold 0.5-1 second for emphasis
- **AI Voice**: Use ElevenLabs, Google Cloud TTS, or record yourself
- **Mix level**: Voice at -6 dB, music at -18 to -20 dB (voice clearly dominant)

## Universal Narration Guidelines

### Words to Emphasize (bold in delivery):
- "Real-time"
- "Every" (every person, every zone, every two seconds)
- "Never" / "No" (never a face, no video stored)
- "Anonymous"
- Numbers (68%, $4.5 billion, 50+ KPIs, $3,500)

### Words to Avoid:
- "Simply" / "Just" (minimizes effort)
- "Revolutionary" / "Game-changing" (hyperbolic)
- "Best" / "Leading" (unsubstantiated superlatives)
- "Cutting-edge" (cliché)

### Pronunciation Guide:
- **YOLO**: "Yoh-loh" (rhymes with "solo")
- **ByteTrack**: "Byte-track" (two words)
- **BoT-SORT**: "Bot-sort" (two words)
- **MJPEG**: "Em-jay-peg"
- **Kalman**: "Kal-man" (as in the filter)
- **MOTA**: "Moh-tah"
- **GDPR**: Spell out: "G-D-P-R"
- **CCPA**: Spell out: "C-C-P-A"
- **BIPA**: "Bye-pah" (not spelled out)
- **KPI**: Spell out: "K-P-I" or say "key performance indicator" first time
